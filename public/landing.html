How it works

Voice note in. Polished work out. Deploy, assign, execute, review.

Four steps between a thought and a sent email. No laptop required. Give every team member an AI agent that works through their existing tools.

01
Speak Connect

Record a 30-second voice note from your phone between meetings or patients. Link your team's existing email, calendar, and file systems in minutes.

02
Context Assign

Outdoors pulls from your laptop: email threads, files, calendar, research papers. Team members text or voice-note tasks to their Everest agent.

03
Polish Execute

Turns your rough thought into professional, ready-to-send work that sounds like you. Each agent drafts work using the employee's real context and files.

04
Send Review

Delivered from your real accounts. Not a draft. Done. You never opened a laptop. Approve and send, or let routine tasks flow automatically.

Calendar intelligence Context-aware agents
